On 13 November 1782, en route to New York, Nelson met Lord Samuel Hood on HMS Barfleur, which was anchored at Staten Island. News of peace terms being discussed, which eventually led to the signing of the Treaty of Versailles on 3 September 1783 and an end to the American War of Independence, meant that HMS Albermarle could return to England. For looking after the Prince, Hood took Nelson as a reward to meet the King on 11 July 1783 at St James Palace. Later Nelson visited the Prince at Windsor.
