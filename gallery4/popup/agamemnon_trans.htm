D[itt]o for the Mozelle to keep sight of the Enemy and Fox Cutter to pass within hail at 16 Minutes past 5 the Admiral made the signal to Engage the Enemy as coming up with them, at 20 Minutes before 6 the signal to Tack together after the close of Day, at 5 Minutes before the signal to Tack together, at ¼ before 8 the Mozelle made the Signal that the Enemy had either Tack or Wore made and shortened Sail 8 signal, the Enemy in Sight AM at 4 all ?
